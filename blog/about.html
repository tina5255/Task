Unleash the wild

Designed this after trying Go&Fun which, was a mistake at 10 PM. Let’s just say sleep wasn’t part of the process. This poster captures the taste of the new flavour and that same burst of energy...minus the 3 AM redecorating spree.

Written by
Martina Fenech
on March 20, 2025

This poster was created in Adobe Photoshop, focusing on bold visuals and an energetic color palette to reflect the vibe of Go&Fun’s new flavour. The design plays with dynamic composition and contrast to capture the drink’s refreshing and exciting personality.

The mockup was sent to Go&Fun, and the brand responded with positive feedback on the concept and overall aesthetic, reinforcing the effectiveness of the design.
